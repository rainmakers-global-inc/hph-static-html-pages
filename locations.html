The Human Powered Health Difference

Our people

Human Powered Health is staffed with Master's and PhD-level performance physiologists who are dedicated to helping you improve your health and performance. Our product is a healthier you, and we have the science to make you better than ever.

Our Technology

Human Powered Health uses high-precision, research-grade, top-of-the-line technology for all assessments in order to provide a superior level of service and quality for anyone who wants to learn more about their health and performance metrics.

Our mission

We are on a mission to power health and human performance for everyone. Your body belongs to you. You deserve accurate information about it. ​We give everyone the data and expertise to fine-tune their own health and athletic performance. Because knowledge is power, and true health is self-made.
