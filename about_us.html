OUR TEAM

DAN COHEN

I am Dan. I am a husband and dad. I am a former professional triathlete. I am an exercise physiologist and coach. I am passionate about discovering human limits – and defying them. I am human powered.

Dan Cohen is the Chief Performance Officer (CPO) for Human Powered Health. As CPO, Dan is responsible for the overall direction and vision of Human Powered Health, the most advanced performance assessment platform, and its mission: to power human potential.

Previously, Dan spent 12 years at UnitedHealth Group where he held leadership positions within UnitedHealth Group Ventures, Rally Health, and Optum Research and Development, identifying, supporting, and managing products and startups. Prior to joining UnitedHealth Group in 2010, Dan was a fitness entrepreneur, founding several human performance companies.

Dan was also a former professional triathlete, duathlete and performance coach.

Dan holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in psychology from Saint Mary’s University and a master’s degree in exercise science from the University of Minnesota. He lives in St. Paul, Minnesota with his wife, Katherine, and two aspiring athletes, Isla (7) and Clyde (5).
