Services for hockey players

results on the ice

Hockey players take note: Human Powered Health can improve your game on the ice, whether you’re a seasoned pro or an aspiring student. We’ve worked with NHL and Division I hockey players to improve their strength, speed and stamina with the power of data applied to training. We can track your aerobic capacity, analyze the content of your sweat, give you data-driven training zones, assess your muscular distribution and reaction time and more. At Human Powered Health, we have precise data and proven results.
