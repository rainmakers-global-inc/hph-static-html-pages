HPH partners with Factor Bikes

Share this post on:

Team will race on Factor’s OSTRO VAM, O2 VAM, and HANZŌ models equipped with Black Inc wheelsets

Human Powered Health will partner with Factor Bikes for the 2024 season and beyond. The team will race atop the OSTRO VAM, O2 VAM, and HANZŌ framesets equipped with Factor’s Black Inc wheelsets.

Human Powered Health will partner with Factor Bikes for the 2024 season and beyond. The team will race atop the OSTRO VAM, O2 VAM, and HANZŌ framesets equipped with Factor’s Black Inc wheelsets.

“We are delighted to partner with Factor Bikes,” said Ro de Jonckere, General Manager “Their bikes are fast, innovative, and sleek. And they are proven at the Women’s WorldTour level. As we grow the team, it’s important to have partners that have the knowledge and experience that can support us all the way.”

In 2023, Factor Bikes raced to victory around the world in the men’s and women’s WorldTours including marquee wins at the Tour de France, Grand Prix Stuttgart and Arctic Race of Norway.

For all-around performance, the fleet includes the OSTRO VAM aero bike, optimized for multiple surfaces, comfortable and super fast whilst remaining light. Then there’s the O2 VAM. The climber bike is just as fast, comfortable and fluid going downhill as it is up the mountains.

Finally, aero perfection has been achieved by the in-house wind tunnel-developed HANZŌ TT bike that pushes every regulation to its limit to achieve maximum performance.

These bikes benefit greatly from Black Inc wheelsets and integrating technologies that bring a new meaning to speed and compliance.

As well as providing the team with the perfect ride, Factor Bikes and Black Inc’s mission aligns with the ethos of Human Powered Health.

“Joining forces with Human Powered Health for the 2024 season is a really exciting opportunity to work with a team whose mission so closely aligns with our own,” said Rob Gitelis, CEO Factor Bikes. “Factor and Black Inc seek to harness cutting-edge innovations and the transformative power of technology to drive each individual to reach their health and performance potential through the power of cycling.”

The three year partnership is an exciting venture for both companies as the team grows from strength to strength in the Women’s WorldTour.

“Human Powered Health’s team is hugely talented and has significant ambitions for the Women’s WorldTour; Factor and Black Inc will be there through every training ride and race to support each rider in unlocking their performance and power them to the top step of the podium,” added Gitelis.
