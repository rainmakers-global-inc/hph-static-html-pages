Strength Your Metabolism

A Resting Metabolic Rate (RMR) test gives you a precise number of calories your body burns per day at rest. Whether you’re looking to lose weight or optimize your health and nutrition, this number is a key element of making informed dietary decisions. You’ll also learn what percentages of calories that you burn at rest are from fat and from carbohydrates — these metrics are critical to assessing and strengthening your metabolism

How

After fasting for 10 hours, you’ll sit comfortably for 30 minutes wearing a heart rate monitor and a special mask that detects variations in the air you exhale. Following the test, you’ll have a 30 minute consultation with a Master’s-level performance physiologist about your unique results and how to improve them.

How Often

Once you’ve had a baseline test, we recommend coming in when you’ve made significant adjustments to your diet or exercise routines
